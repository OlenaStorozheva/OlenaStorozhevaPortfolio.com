About

Turning workflow complexity into clarity

I'm a UX/UI and Product Designer based in Ukraine, specializing in B2B SaaS and enterprise platforms. For the past 4+ years at Nix Solutions, I've owned the end-to-end design of an RPA platform — a product where precision, accessibility, and design-system discipline aren't optional extras, they're the baseline.

My approach is rooted in systems thinking: I don't just design screens, I design the logic that connects them. Whether I'm mapping task flows, running usability tests, or refining component libraries in Figma, I'm always asking: "How does this fit the system, and does it make someone's workday easier?"

I work fluidly across cross-functional teams — translating requirements from stakeholders and product managers into high-fidelity, developer-ready designs. I take feedback well, iterate fast, and can plan and monitor my own workstreams without needing constant direction.

Before UX, I spent years in graphic and brand design. That foundation shows up in my attention to visual craft — the kind of detail that separates a good interface from a memorable one.

Selected projects

01 · Lead Project
RPA Platform UX — Digital Workforce Management
Problem Enterprise users managing complex RPA workflows through a dense, unintuitive interface — high cognitive load, steep onboarding curve for non-technical operators.
Solution Redesigned the information architecture and component system in Figma, establishing a scalable design system that unified workflows across the platform. Introduced progressive disclosure patterns and accessible UI for data-heavy dashboards.
Impact Shipped across multiple releases; pixel-perfect handoff reduced dev QA cycles. Accessibility compliance brought the platform to W3C standards.
Figma Design System B2B SaaS Accessibility RPA
02
Workflow Automation — Task Flow Redesign
Problem Operators creating and managing automated workflows lacked clear visual feedback on execution states, causing errors and requiring developer intervention.
Solution Mapped end-to-end task flows with stakeholders, designed a new state-machine visualization with clear status indicators. Used interactive Figma prototypes to validate with users before development.
Impact Prototype-first approach aligned developers and product managers early, reducing design revision rounds and accelerating handoff.
Figma Prototyping Task Flows Enterprise UX
