About me

I'm a UX/UI and Product Designer specialising in B2B SaaS and enterprise platforms. For the past 4+ years at Nix Solutions, I've owned the end-to-end design of an RPA platform — a product where precision, accessibility, and design-system discipline are the baseline, not the bonus.

My approach is grounded in systems thinking: I don't just design screens, I design the logic connecting them. Whether mapping task flows, running usability tests, or refining component libraries in Figma, I'm always asking: how does this fit the system — and does it make someone's workday meaningfully better?

I collaborate fluently across cross-functional teams — translating abstract requirements from stakeholders and product managers into high-fidelity, developer-ready designs. I iterate fast, take feedback constructively, and can plan and monitor my own workstreams independently.

Before UX, I spent years in graphic and brand design. That foundation still shows up in my visual craft — the kind of considered detail that separates a functional interface from a truly memorable one.
